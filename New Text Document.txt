Task Tracker:
Description:
Having lot of tasks to do?? But not organized any of them?? But here is a solution to arrange your tasks. Now you can organize & categorize your to-do list with the help of this application. You can also share your files by using SkyDrive. 
	
Features:
•	Plan your tasks & todos to achieve what you want to do.
•	You can have the tracking the achievement of tasks.
•	Share files with SkyDrive.
•	Add Categories.
•	Add Items.
•	Edit, Delete the items or Categories.
•	Fully Different User Interface Designed keeping in focus point of view of Niche (Target) Market.
•	Fully Unique Branding (Unique Logo Designing & Graphics Designing)
